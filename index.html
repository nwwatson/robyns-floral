About Robyn's Floral

Robyn's Floral and Baloons specializes in custom floral masterpieces.

Custom Floral Creations

Located within Duncan True Value on Lowery Avenue in Jeannette, Robyn's Floral and Baloons provides custom floral arrangements and services for Westmoreland county and the surrounding areas. Each arrangement is completely custom, reflecting the tastes of each individual customer.

Robyn's Floral is a full service florist. Some of the services we provide are:

  • Custom flower arrangements
  • Baloons
  • Gifts
  • Weddings and Events
  • Delivery
